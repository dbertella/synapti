Mission

        In the Lab we carry out Research activities, Workshop, 
        Conferences, Design of Exhibition for art works 
        inside museums, art galleries and Exhibitions
        and top range commercial products
        
Reserch:

        Through an interdisciplinary approach,based on the self-generating principle of neural networks,  
        works by great artists of ages and places deliberately different from each other are elaborated, 
        in order to spot possible common expressive mechanisms in their languages, 
        considered as part of a sub-symbolic language, which operate underneath  the stylistic
        and historic-cultural configurations of their works.
    
          Research has pointed out that the artists analyzed 
          (among whom  Leonardo, Tiziano Mirò, Magritte, Beuys, Weston, Pesce, Piano, Cattelan, Hirst and others)
          have  intuitively created some sub-symbolic mechanisms in their works, which have 
          determined their success over time. In fact the sub-symbolic language is “automatically” 
          elaborated by the mind and the body ( embodied mind ) of those who live the aesthetic experience 
          both as an artist and as an observer. This natural language is therefore produced 
          and understood by each individual  according to a biopsychic code, which had never been 
          revealed before in the field of art.
