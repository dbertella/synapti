Mission

      In the Lab we carry out research activities,
      workshops, conferences and design exhibition spaces
       for displaying art works and top range commercial products.

        
Reserch:

        Through an interdisciplinary approach,based on the self-generating principle
        of neural networks, we elaborate  works by great artists of ages and places
        deliberately different from each other  in order to spot in their languages possible
        common  structures, meant as mechanisms of a sub-symbolic language,
        which operate underneath  the stylistic and historic-cultural configurations of such works.
      
      Research has pointed out that the artists analyzed (among whom  Leonardo, Tiziano, Mirò, Magritte,
      Beuys, Weston, Pesce, Piano, Cattelan, Hirst, Quinn and others) have  intuitively created
      some sub-symbolic mechanisms in their works, which have  determined their  undisputed success over time.
      In fact the sub-symbolic language is “automatically” elaborated by the mind and the body ( embodied mind )
      of those who live the aesthetic experience both as an artist and as an observer.
      This natural language is therefore produced and understood by each individual 
      according to a biopsychic code, which has never been revealed before in the field of art.
