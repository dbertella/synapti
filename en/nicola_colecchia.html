Nicola Colecchia

      Neuropsychologist, as one of the main 
      experts in dynamic systems and 
      neural networks applied to visual arts, 
      he has been Lecturer and Researcher 
       at the University of Bologna and the Facuty of Medicine 
      and Surgery, University of Chieti. 
www.nicolacolecchia.it
      
 

              Using the neural modeling he has proved that the success
              of a work of art is determined by biopsychic mechanisms 
              underlying the stylistic configurations and cultural-history 
              of the work examined.
       
              
                                              He is the Director, Author 
                                              and Content Manager of SynapticArt, 
                                              Museum of Art and Science - Milan.
